Productivity

Okay, get ready. I try and use processes that help me break up tasks into smaller pieces

  • Pomodoros: I use the pomodoro technique when I am having a lot of trouble focusing.
  • Field Notes: These are pocket sized notebooks that I like to use to have a physical to-do pad and a place to put day-to-day into.
  • Productive: I use this to create daily/weekly tasks. This helps keep track of tasks I want to continue to do every day like spending time reading or learning a new technology.
  • Trello: What I use to manage my side projects and organize information and resources. One of the best free softwares. Well-designed and lightweight.
